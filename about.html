At The 3rd Dimension Aeromodelling Club, we engage ourselves to design and develop a span of models varying from Aerodynamic gliders to Autonomous Drones.
We take up projects that unfold new areas related to aeromodelling, fluid mechanics, surveillance, advanced control systems, and auto-piloting algorithms, which set us new records.
The club was founded in 2013 by many aeromodelling enthusiasts who had a passion for defying gravity and soaring high by building UAVs from scratch. Over the years, our models have been evolved a lot, our domain has expanded wide, and we have secured many podiums in different National Competitions and events.

About Us

The Third dimension is the aeromodelling club of NITT and primarily deals with, as the name goes, motion in the Third dimension-the principles of defying gravity and achieving flight.
